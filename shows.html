What happens when the perfect husband, the devoted wife, and an unexpected "Oh Terri" moment collide in the same living room? Pure, unadulterated chaos — and comedy gold. Pati, Patni, aur "Oh Terri" is a love letter to every married couple who has ever had a catastrophically bad day together and survived to laugh about it.

Performed entirely in Hindi with live English supertitles, this production is crafted for the entire Bay Area — desi and desi-adjacent alike. Expect sharp writing, expressive performances, and one or two moments that will make you nudge your partner in the ribs.

"No heavy drama. Just good stories, great people, and a lot of laughter."

— The Desi Theatre Company
● Doors open 30 minutes before showtime. Light refreshments available in the foyer. Please arrive on time — the curtain rises promptly.
