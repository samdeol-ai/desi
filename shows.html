Pati, Patni, aur “Oh Terri”

A riotous comedy about love, marriage, and the spectacular chaos that lives in between. Five shows only.

Pati, Patni, aur “Oh Terri”

A Hindi comedy naatak with English supertitles , Spring 2026

Director
Niranjan Page
Producer
Nitin Deo
Set Design
Chintan Vora
Costume Design
Aditi Honawar
English Supertitles
Arti Raina-Bambroo
Props
Mousumi Chaudhuri

What happens when the perfect husband, the devoted wife, and an unexpected “Oh Terri” moment collide in the same living room? Pure, unadulterated chaos, and comedy gold. A love letter to every married couple who has survived a catastrophically bad day together and laughed about it.

Performed entirely in Hindi with live English supertitles, crafted for the entire Bay Area, desi and desi-adjacent alike.

“No heavy drama. Just good stories, great people, and a lot of laughter.”

, The Desi Theatre Company
●  Doors open 30 minutes before showtime. Light refreshments in the foyer. Curtain rises promptly.
