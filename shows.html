Pati, Patni, aur “Oh Terri”

A riotous comedy about love, marriage, and the spectacular chaos that lives in between. Five shows only.

Pati, Patni, aur “Oh Terri”

A Hindi comedy naatak with English supertitles — Spring 2026

What happens when the perfect husband, the devoted wife, and an unexpected “Oh Terri” moment collide in the same living room? Pure, unadulterated chaos — and comedy gold. This is a love letter to every married couple who has survived a catastrophically bad day together and laughed about it.

Performed entirely in Hindi with live English supertitles — crafted for the entire Bay Area, desi and desi-adjacent alike.

“No heavy drama. Just good stories, great people, and a lot of laughter.”

— The Desi Theatre Company
●  Doors open 30 minutes before showtime. Light refreshments in the foyer. Curtain rises promptly — please arrive on time.
