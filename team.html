Founding Members

The original six.

In 2024, six friends with a shared love for Hindi theatre, abundant chai, and an audacious plan sat down and decided to build a community stage from scratch. This is who started it all.

M

Maitreyi

Co-Founder

One of the original six who decided that the Bay Area needed a proper Hindi comedy theatre and then actually did something about it. A creative force and community connector.

The Desi Theatre Spirit

Powered by passion, not the script alone.

Every person involved in a Desi Theatre production is a community volunteer who turns up because theatre is their happy place. From lead performer to the person who brings the samosas on rehearsal night, every role is owned by someone who genuinely wants to be there.

That energy is impossible to fake. And it is what makes Desi Theatre shows feel different the moment you walk in the door.

In Collab

Guest artists in our green room.

Great productions often call for a special skill that deserves a specialist. Our In Collab roster includes invited artists and professionals from outside Desi Theatre who lend their craft to specific productions.

They are not permanent members of the company but are very much part of the family when they are with us. Think of them as the star guest who always gets a standing ovation.

Interested in collaborating? We welcome approaches from directors, choreographers, musicians, lighting designers, and other theatre practitioners.
