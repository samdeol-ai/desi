2024
Where It All Began

Born from a simple idea: what if theatre felt like a holiday?

It started with a living room conversation between a handful of Bay Area friends who missed the energy of desi theatre — the kind they grew up watching, the kind that made entire families laugh until their sides hurt.

The Bay Area has one of the largest South Asian communities in the United States. What it didn't have — at least not in the way it deserved — was a dedicated Hindi comedy theatre company producing high-quality naatak year-round.

So a group of passionate volunteers decided to build one. No corporate backing. No government grants. Just people who loved theatre, loved their community, and loved a good laugh. Desi Theatre Company was born.

Vision & Mission

✨
Our Vision

In a world of constant hustle, we offer a space where stress fades and laughter takes over.

Every individual feels at home celebrating life, culture, and community through lighthearted theatre. That is the world Desi Theatre is working to build — one naatak at a time.

🏆
Our Mission

To bring people together through high-quality, creative theatre that delivers joy.

Without ever compromising on craft. We are a community theatre, yes — but "community" is never an excuse for anything less than excellent. From the set to the script, every element is handled with care and heart.

The Journey

Three seasons. One growing story.

’24

Season 1 — The Debut

Desi Theatre's founding production. A short-form comedy showcase that introduced the Bay Area to a fresh, energetic voice in Hindi theatre.

’25

Season 2 — Caught in the Net

A full-length naatak exploring the chaos of modern family life in the age of social media. Bigger cast, bigger laughs, bigger ambitions.

’26

Season 3 — Pati, Patni, aur “Oh Terri”

Our most polished production yet. Historic Hoover Theater. Five shows. 15+ volunteers. A story that will make you call your partner on the way home.

What We Stand For

Six values. One unforgettable evening.

Every production, every rehearsal, every backstage moment is guided by these six principles. They aren't posters on a wall — they're the reason people keep coming back.

01

Comedy

Comedy is at the heart of everything. We strive to create fun content for the Hindi-speaking audience — sometimes meaningful, sometimes meaningless. Always funny.

02

Community

Promoting cultural diversity, integrity, and harmony in the Bay Area desi community. Theatre as a meeting point. A shared laugh as a shared identity.

03

Welcome

Non-judgmental. Respectful. Focused on fun and food. Desi Theatre strives to be a space where everyone — from first-timers to veterans — feels at home.

04

Inclusion

An inclusive, neutral, and joyful culture across all production aspects. English supertitles ensure the story reaches everyone in the room, regardless of background.

05

Vibrancy

We seek vibrant, enthusiastic, and diverse volunteers. Energy is contagious — and the best performances come from people who genuinely want to be on that stage.

06

Empowerment

Desi Theatre passionately champions women empowerment — onstage, backstage, and in every leadership role within the company. The spotlight belongs to everyone.
