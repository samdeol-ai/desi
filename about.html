Our Story

We're not just here to put on plays.
We're here to give you a break.

Founded in the Bay Area by passionate desi theatre lovers. Built on laughs, culture, and community.

2024 Where It All Began

Born from a simple idea: what if theatre felt like a holiday?

It started with a living room conversation between a handful of Bay Area friends who missed the energy of desi theatre, the kind they grew up watching, the kind that made entire families laugh until their sides hurt.

The Bay Area has one of the largest South Asian communities in the United States. What it didn't have was a dedicated Hindi comedy theatre company producing high-quality naatak year-round.

So a group of passionate volunteers decided to build one. No corporate backing. No government grants. Just people who loved theatre, loved their community, and loved a good laugh.

Desi Theatre Company was born.

Our North Star

Vision & Mission

✨
Our Vision

In a world of constant hustle, our vision is to offer a space where stress fades, laughter takes over, and every individual feels at home celebrating life, culture, and community through lighthearted theatre.

🏆
Our Mission

To bring people together through high-quality, creative theatre that delivers joy without ever compromising on craft.

From set to script, costumes to supertitles, every element of a Desi Theatre production is handled with care, creativity, and a whole lot of heart.

The Journey

Three seasons. One growing story.

’24

Season 1 , The Debut

Desi Theatre's founding production. A short-form comedy showcase that introduced the Bay Area to a fresh, energetic voice in Hindi theatre.

’25

Season 2 , Caught in the Net

A full-length naatak exploring the chaos of modern family life in the age of social media. Bigger cast, bigger laughs, bigger ambitions.

’26

Season 3 , Pati, Patni, aur “Oh Terri”

Our most polished production yet. Historic Hoover Theater. Five shows. 15+ volunteers. A story that will make you call your partner on the way home.

What We Stand For

Six values. One unforgettable evening.

01

Comedy

Comedy is at the heart of everything. Sometimes meaningful, sometimes gloriously meaningless. Always funny.

02

Community

Promoting cultural diversity, integrity, and harmony in the Bay Area desi community. Theatre as a meeting point.

03

Welcome

Non-judgmental. Respectful. Focused on fun and food. A space where everyone feels at home, first-timers and veterans alike.

04

Inclusion

An inclusive, neutral, joyful culture across all production aspects. English supertitles ensure the story reaches everyone.

05

Vibrancy

We seek vibrant, enthusiastic, diverse volunteers. The best performances come from people who genuinely want to be on that stage.

06

Empowerment

Desi Theatre passionately champions women empowerment, on stage, backstage, and in every leadership role within the company.
