Auditions

Open. Year-round. For everyone.

Desi Theatre runs a rolling auditions model. Whether it is March or October, if you are ready, so are we. No cold read surprises. No panel of intimidating judges. Just you, a room, and a story to tell.

01

Register Your Interest

Fill in the contact form or email us directly. We will add you to the auditions list and let you know when the next round opens.

02

Prepare a Monologue

A two to three minute Hindi monologue of your choice. The subject does not matter. What matters is your energy, your presence, and the spark you bring to the room.

03

Step Into the Light

Walk in, own the space, and show us who you are. We are rooting for you before you have said your first word. Seriously, we want you to do well.

04

Hear from Us

Every auditionee gets a personal response. Whatever the outcome, we value the courage it takes to show up and perform. That deserves acknowledgement.
