Auditions

Open. Year-Round. For everyone.

Desi Theatre runs on a rolling auditions model. Whether it's March or October — if you're ready, so are we.

01

Register Your Interest

Fill in the contact form or email us directly. We'll add you to the auditions list and let you know when the next round is scheduled.

02

Prepare a Monologue

A 2–3 minute Hindi monologue of your choice. The subject doesn't matter — it's about your energy, presence, and commitment.

03

Come In & Bring It

Walk into the room, own the space, and show us who you are. We're rooting for you before you've said your first word.

04

We'll Be in Touch

Regardless of outcome, every auditionee receives a personalised response. We value your courage and your time.
