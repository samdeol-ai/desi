Auditions are open.

This could be your moment. We're looking for expression, confidence, and presence — not a CV or a showreel. Desi Theatre welcomes first-timers and experienced performers alike.

Auditions are held on a rolling basis ahead of each production. Register your interest and we'll notify you when the next round is announced.

Register for Audition →

How Auditions Work

1

Register Your Interest

Fill in the contact form or email us. We'll add you to the auditions list for the next production.

2

Prepare a Short Monologue

A 2–3 minute Hindi monologue of your choice. The subject doesn't matter — your energy does.

3

Come In & Bring It

Walk into the room, own the space, and show us who you are. We're rooting for you before you've said a word.

4

We'll Be in Touch

Regardless of outcome, every auditionee gets a response. We value your time and your courage.
