## Tips for Photography

- **Format:** JPG (optimised, 80–85% quality) for all photos
- **Colour grading:** Warm tones — amber, gold, burgundy. Avoid cool/blue grading.
- **Hero images:** Should be dark on the left-side (index, join) or bottom (about, shows) so white text remains legible without extra overlay
- **File size target:** Hero images under 400KB, gallery images under 150KB. Use [Squoosh](https://squoosh.app) or [TinyJPG](https://tinyjpg.com) to compress.
- **Minimum resolution:** All images should be at least 1.5× the display size for Retina/HiDPI screens
